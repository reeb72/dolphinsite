A brief history on dolphins:

Marine Mammals which are referred to as Cetaceans, born 50 million years ago in the Eocene epoch. Once they used to be animals that walked, but now they are in the sea?!?!?

AMAZING Dolphin facts:

  1. There are 36+ species of marine dolphins, together they occupy most environments
  2. They can reach speeds of over 30 mph for brief periods!
  3. They swallow a fish head first so the fish's spines don't catch in their throat.
  4. They use their tail to hit fish and so stun them making them easier to catch.
  5. Dolphins have some of the most elaborate acoustic abilities in the animal kingdom. They make a variety of sounds including whistles, clicks, squawks, squeaks, moans, barks, groans and yelps.
  6. Dolphins are alledgedly the second smartest animal, only second to monkeys
  7. THEY CAN TURN OFF HALF OF THEIR BRAIN???
